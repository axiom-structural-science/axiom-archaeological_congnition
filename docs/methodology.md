# Research Methodology

## Data Collection
- Archaeological sites selected from peer-reviewed publications
- Direct artifact examination and published analyses
- Multidisciplinary verification of interpretations

## Concept Mapping
- 12 primal concepts identified through cross-cultural analysis
- Each concept mapped to specific symbolic evidence
- Evolutionary sequence established through rigorous dating

## Analysis Framework
- AXIOM structural analysis for cognitive complexity
- STI (Structural Truth Index) scoring adapted for archaeology
- Comparative analysis across sites and time periods
